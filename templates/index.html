What is this?

CryptoMeter is a software designed to help traders know what to expect. This software will give you insight to the current state of cryptocurrencies, and what the predicted prices are going to be tomorrow, so that you can buy or sell accordingly. Please note that this is NOT FINANCIAL ADVICE.

How does it work?

At CryptoMeter, we understand the connection between public perception and cryptocurrencies value. We have designed a model to relate the sentiment score of thousands of tweets related to cryptocurrencies, and related this to the price of cryptocurrencies.

About Us

We are a group of students from the University of Toronto. We have a passion for cryptocurrencies and technology. We have developed this to help others passionate about crypto, just like us.
